Complete Design-Build Service

Bali-Specific Expertise

Installing pools in Bali requires understanding local soil conditions, building codes, and tropical climate considerations. We expertly handle challenges like high water tables, volcanic rock substrates, monsoon drainage requirements, and integration with Balinese architectural aesthetics.

Eco-Friendly Installation

Environmental responsibility guides our installation practices. We incorporate energy-efficient equipment, LED lighting, solar heating options, water-saving features, and sustainable construction materials that reduce operating costs while preserving Bali's environment.

The Pool Installation Process

A typical residential pool installation in Bali requires 8-12 weeks from excavation to completion. Our systematic approach ensures exceptional results: 1) Design consultation and site assessment. 2) Engineering and permitting (1-2 weeks). 3) Excavation and site preparation (1-2 weeks). 4) Plumbing and electrical installation (3-5 days). 5) Steel reinforcement and shell construction (1-2 weeks). 6) Curing period (7-10 days). 7) Surface finishing - plaster, pebble, or tile (1-2 weeks). 8) Equipment installation and startup (1 week). 9) Deck construction and landscaping (2-3 weeks). Throughout your project, you'll have a dedicated project manager ensuring smooth progress and regular communication.

Pool Types We Install

We specialize in various pool configurations suited to Bali's environment: Infinity pools showcasing spectacular views, saltwater pools for softer water and easier maintenance, natural pools using biological filtration, lap pools for fitness enthusiasts, plunge pools perfect for smaller properties, and resort-style commercial pools. Each type has unique engineering and aesthetic considerations that our experienced team handles expertly.
