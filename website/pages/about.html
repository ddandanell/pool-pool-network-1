Our Story: From Small Beginnings to Bali's Leading Pool Service

Fifteen years of excellence in pool maintenance, cleaning, repair, and installation across Bali

The Foundation of EcoPool Bali

EcoPool Bali was founded in 2010 by a passionate group of local Balinese pool technicians and expatriate engineering professionals who recognized a critical gap in Bali's rapidly growing hospitality and residential property markets. At that time, the island was experiencing unprecedented development with luxury villas, boutique resorts, and upscale residences sprouting across regions from Seminyak to Ubud. However, quality pool service in Bali remained inconsistent, with most property owners struggling to find reliable, knowledgeable professionals who understood both modern pool technology and the unique challenges of Bali's tropical environment.

Our co-founders, I Made Sudana and Australian engineer David Mitchell, first met while working on a large resort development in Sanur. I Made brought decades of experience in Bali's construction industry and intimate knowledge of local water conditions, while David contributed expertise in advanced pool systems and water treatment technology. Together, they identified an opportunity to create a pool service company that would combine traditional Balinese work ethic and customer service with cutting-edge pool maintenance techniques and eco-friendly practices.

Starting with just two technicians and a single service vehicle, EcoPool Bali began operations serving a handful of villas in the Ubud area. Our early clients were primarily villa owners who had experienced frustration with unreliable pool maintenance services, green algae-filled pools despite regular cleaning, and equipment failures due to improper maintenance. Word of our thorough, professional approach to pool service Bali spread quickly through the tight-knit expatriate and hospitality communities.

Growth and Expansion

By 2012, demand for our pool maintenance Bali services had grown so substantially that we expanded our team to eight technicians and added two additional service vehicles. We also established our first dedicated office and parts warehouse in Gianyar, strategically located to serve both the Ubud highlands and coastal areas efficiently. This expansion allowed us to offer faster response times for pool repair Bali emergencies and maintain a comprehensive inventory of replacement parts for all major pool equipment brands.

The period from 2013 to 2015 marked significant growth as we branched into swimming pool installation Bali services. Recognizing that many of the pools we serviced had been poorly designed or constructed without consideration for Bali's tropical climate, we decided to offer complete pool design and installation services. Our first major installation project was a stunning 20-meter infinity pool at a cliff-side villa in Pecatu, which won recognition from local architectural publications and established our reputation for quality pool construction.

By 2016, EcoPool Bali had grown to become one of the island's largest pool service providers, maintaining over 200 residential and commercial pools across Bali. We expanded our service areas to include all major regions of Bali including Canggu, Seminyak, Sanur, Nusa Dua, Jimbaran, and Tabanan. Our team had grown to 25 professionals including certified pool technicians, engineers, installation specialists, and customer service staff.

Innovation and Environmental Leadership

In 2017, we made the strategic decision to fully embrace eco-friendly pool service practices, becoming one of the first pool maintenance companies in Indonesia to prioritize environmental sustainability. This shift wasn't just about marketing; it reflected a genuine commitment to protecting Bali's precious environment. We partnered with international eco-pool technology companies to introduce saltwater pool conversions, mineral pool systems, and energy-efficient variable-speed pumps to the Bali market.

Our environmental initiatives expanded to include exclusive use of biodegradable cleaning products, implementation of water conservation techniques that reduced pool water waste by up to 40%, installation of LED pool lighting systems, promotion of solar pool heating, and development of chemical management protocols that minimized environmental impact while maintaining superior water quality. These innovations resonated strongly with environmentally conscious villa owners and resort managers, further solidifying our position as leaders in pool service Bali.

Recognition and Achievements

Throughout our journey, EcoPool Bali has achieved numerous milestones that we're immensely proud of. In 2018, we received the Bali Hospitality Excellence Award for Outstanding Pool Service Provider, recognizing our contribution to the island's tourism industry. We've completed over 150 swimming pool installation projects, including some of Bali's most photographed and admired pools. Our portfolio includes installations for award-winning resorts, celebrity-owned villas, and exclusive private estates.

In 2019, we established a comprehensive training program for pool technicians, becoming the first pool service company in Bali to offer certified training in cooperation with international pool associations. This program not only elevated our team's expertise but also contributed to raising professional standards across Bali's pool service industry. We've trained over 50 technicians, many of whom have gone on to establish their own businesses or work for major resort properties.

The COVID-19 pandemic of 2020-2021 presented unprecedented challenges as Bali's tourism industry ground to a halt. However, our diverse client base including residential properties and our reputation for reliability helped us weather the storm. We actually grew during this period as homeowners, spending more time at their properties, invested in pool upgrades and prioritized regular pool maintenance Bali services to create private resort experiences at home.

EcoPool Bali Today

Today, EcoPool Bali stands as Bali's premier pool service provider with a team of 35+ experienced professionals serving over 500 clients across the island. We handle everything from routine weekly pool maintenance for small residential pools to complete pool management for large resort properties with multiple pools. Our services encompass comprehensive pool maintenance Bali programs, professional pool cleaning services, expert pool repair for all equipment and structural issues, custom swimming pool installation from design to completion, pool renovation and upgrading, saltwater and mineral pool conversions, and energy-efficiency improvements.

We've invested significantly in technology, utilizing digital service management systems that provide clients with detailed service reports, water chemistry tracking, equipment maintenance histories, and automated scheduling. Our fleet of fully-equipped service vehicles ensures we can respond to pool repair Bali emergencies anywhere on the island within 2-4 hours. Our parts warehouse stocks thousands of components, enabling same-day repairs in most situations.

Our Commitment to Excellence

What sets EcoPool Bali apart isn't just our technical expertise or comprehensive services; it's our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and environmental responsibility. Every member of our team undergoes rigorous training and certification. We maintain strict quality control standards with regular supervisor inspections and client feedback systems. Our transparent pricing model ensures clients know exactly what they're paying for, with no hidden fees or unnecessary upsells.

We're proud to have built lasting relationships with our clients, many of whom have been with us since our early days. These relationships are built on trust, reliability, and consistent delivery of exceptional pool service in Bali. Whether maintaining a small villa pool or installing a complex resort pool system, we bring the same dedication to excellence and attention to detail that has defined EcoPool Bali since 2010.

Looking forward, we remain committed to innovation, sustainability, and maintaining our position as Bali's most trusted pool service provider. We're continuously exploring new eco-friendly technologies, expanding our service capabilities, and training the next generation of pool professionals. Our goal is simple: to ensure that every pool we touch becomes a source of joy, relaxation, and pride for its owners while minimizing environmental impact and maximizing longevity.
